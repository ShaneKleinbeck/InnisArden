01 | The Store

The recent releases to the classics that never die


Innis Arden has collected vinyl from every genere imaginable -- we pride ourselves on collecting everything we cna get our hands on. if wee dont have what you are looking for we will go out and find it.


State of the art equipment


Every visit to our store is an experience. We invite you to lounge around, drink freshly brewed coffee, and enjoy your favorite vinyl. All the equipment is top of the line to bring you the ultimate musical pleasure.


If you visit be sure to take a look at the art on our walls - they are made by local artists and most are for sale. We strongly believe in supporting local business and individuals.

Largest record store in the northwestern United States


Our unique usage of a rented duplex allows us to create an environment that is conducive to creativity and warmth. Records for sale span both floors while their hardware counterparts are stationed in specific areas all themed to a genre. The coffee bar has plenty of seating for those wishing to relax and enjoy conversations with those around them.
